Light and Dark, 2018

When I create these projects, I try to gain inspiration from various media such as video games and anime and try to create a concept that is unique to my artwork. Despite being a beginning artist, I am proud of how my projects have turned out and plan to improve on future projects and learn from them.

I create these projects with Adobe Photoshop or Adobe Premiere. I was able to learn how to use the software while creating these projects and I plan to use these skill in future projects and expand my knowledge.
