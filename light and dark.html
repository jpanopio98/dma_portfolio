Light and Dark

Digital Image, 2018

This was my very first digital media art piece. My concept for this piece was to take an image of Anakin Skywalker and Darth Vader and overlay them on top of each other to symbolize Anakin's struggle between light and dark. I edited this image in Adobe Photoshop and used various tools to make the glitch effect to symbolize that struggle between light and dark.
