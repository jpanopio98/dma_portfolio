Naruto AMV:For My Friend

Anime Music Video, 2018

This particular piece has been something I worked on for months and spent a lot of time editing. My inspiration for this work was from Anime Music Videos I see on YouTube and my love for the anime Naruto. I gathered footage of moments in the anime and pushed my editing skills to its limits with jump cuts and various effects added.
